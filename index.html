Our Mission

Are you tired of wasting your time and resources on tax filing? Have you relied on TurboTaxes expensive and slow services? Well we have the solution for you!

Toad Tax is an innovative and free website that takes in your tax information and produces a downloadable pdf of the 1040 form. Our website doesn't have a database installed so none of your sensitive information will be saved or tracked!
